Overview

Ray Foundation is a mission-driven organization focused on improving lives through education, healthcare, clean water access, and community development. My involvement gave me the chance to contribute to a meaningful cause while supporting work that creates visible impact.

My Role

  • Helped with fundraising-related tasks and donation support efforts.
  • Worked alongside Raymond, the CEO of the foundation.
  • Supported the organization through practical collaboration and contribution.
  • Started creating a modern website to strengthen the foundation’s online presence.

Personal Connection

This work is meaningful to me because both Raymond and I come from the same hometown and country, Sierra Leone. That shared background made the mission feel more personal and strengthened my desire to contribute in a way that goes beyond technical work.

What I Contributed

  • Supported donation-focused efforts that help fund real community work.
  • Contributed time and effort to help the organization move its mission forward.
  • Applied design and development skills to improve the foundation’s visibility.
  • Helped connect technical contribution with a meaningful community-centered purpose.

Website Contribution

One of the strongest parts of my contribution is building a nice, modern website presence for the foundation. This helps the organization communicate its mission more clearly, present its work more professionally, and improve how supporters connect with the cause online.

What this proves I can contribute in real-world environments, support nonprofit and fundraising efforts, collaborate with leadership, and apply technical skills in ways that create impact beyond classroom projects.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Fundraising support
  • Leadership collaboration
  • Community-oriented thinking

Why It Matters

This project adds a different kind of strength to my portfolio. It shows that I can work not only on analytics and technical systems, but also on mission-driven, people-centered work that requires trust, initiative, and real responsibility.
